About Us

Shalima Electricals was formed in 2018. Over the years our sole objective has been to become specialists in what we believe to be the more interesting side of electrical distribution. Since that time we have become one of the best Electrical shops in Tiruppur. Shalima Electricals' growth and vision have always been in sync with the idea of a one-stop solution for all Electrical needs



We are engaged in distributing superior quality Electricals Products, which are procured from distinguished manufacturers such as Legrand, RR Kabel, Finolex, E-Fab, Crompton, Orient, GM, Polycab, Astral, etc.,
